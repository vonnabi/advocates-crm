# Glossary

- Client: person or organization receiving legal services.
- Case / Matter: legal work unit connected to a client.
- Document: file, draft, request, claim, invoice, act, or case material.
- Deadline: date/time requiring action.
- Task: work item assigned to a user.
- Invoice: payment request for services.
- Payment: incoming or outgoing finance operation.
- Responsible lawyer: main user responsible for case progress.
- Case stage: current procedural or business stage.
- Court: judicial authority.
- Claim: formal legal demand or lawsuit material.
- Contract: agreement between parties.
